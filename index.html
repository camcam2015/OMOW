One Month, One Website

an experiment in consistency

Sheesh I don't know what to put here. Of course, as an extremely new website that's not even published yet (just hosted on GitHub) it's not like what I'll say now matters to anyone. To the me of the future: I believe you can do this. This website *technically* counts as your first, so good job! You're one down.
